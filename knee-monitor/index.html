Knee Monitor for physiotherapists

A post-surgery rehab wearable that was failing in the field. On-site research showed the design had been built for the wrong user — surgeons, not the physiotherapists actually using it day to day. Re-labelled sensors and an onboarding flow designed for physios cleared the complaint stream and unblocked the launch. The walkthrough below is the screen-by-screen tour of what shipped.
